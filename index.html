    1. Schedule for Tantrash 1.0 will be on 1-2 September 2018
    2. Tantransh 1.0 will be having 3 independent categories as Lollipop, Kitkat and Oreo
    3. Only group entry is allowed and maximum 4 members are permitted in a group
    4. A group is allowed to participate in any one of the category mentioned above
    5. Under each category maximum 30 groups are allowed, registration to corresponding category will be closed once it reaches the limit
    6. Registration is open for all branches and for all semesters of GHRCE, Nagpur
    7. Cash Prices
      1. Lollipop
        2. Winner - Rs.3000    Runner Up - Rs.1500
      2. Kitkat
        3. Winner - Rs.5000    Runner Up - Rs.2500
      3. Oreo
        4. Winner - Rs.7500    Runner Up - Rs.3500
    8. Time Span given for Implementation of Software is as follows
      1. Lollipop     08 hrs.
      2. Kitkat      22 hrs.
      3. Oreo      30 hrs.
    9. Last Date of Registration is 25 August 2018.
    10. Evaluation of the prototype / Software will be done by Director GHRCE, Industry Experts and Concerned Cell In-charges
    11. You will be eligible for cash prize only when you show fully working model which satisfy all constraints and functionalities
    12. There will be evaluation under each category, which is as follows
      1. Lollipop- First Evaluation after 2 hrs, Second After 5 hrs and Third Evaluation at 08 hrs
      2. Kitkat- First Evaluation after 4 hrs, Second After 10 hrs, Third Evaluation at 22 hrs
      3. Oreo- First Evaluation after 5 hrs, Second After 12 hrs, Third Evaluation at 24 hrs and Final Evaluation is at 30 hrs

    14. You will be eligible for participation certificate if your group stays for at-least 2nd rounds of evaluation. If group decide to leave early, it is allowed but no participation certificate would be issued
    15. Accommodation and necessary food arrangement will be the responsibility of college
